Chicken Tikka

The dish is popular in India, Bangladesh and Pakistan.
It is traditionally small pieces of boneless chicken baked using skewers on a brazier called Tikka
or over charcoal after marinating in Indian spices and yogurtâ€”essentially a boneless version of tandoori chicken.

Ingredients for chicken tikka

  • Chicken
  • Butter
  • Chili Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Yogurt
  • Cream
  • Tomato
  • Indian Spices
  • Coriander
  • Ginger
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Cumin
  • Garlic Ginger Paste

Steps to make Chicken Tikka

  1. Take fresh boneless chicken in the bowl
  2. Add salt in it
  3. Add sum amount of Garlic Ginger paste
  4. Add oil
  5. Add Chili pepper into chicken
  6. Add yogurt into chicken
  7. Add Indian Spices into it
  8. Add sum amount of Tomato and Onion
  9. Keep it for 30 minutes to mix all the spices to the chicken
  10. Now keep chicken into Oven
  11. Your chicken tikka is ready to eat
