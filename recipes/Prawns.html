Ginger Prawns

Prawns are a versatile ingredient that can be cooked using a range of methods
- poaching, grilling, pan-frying or as a tempura are all common techniques.

Ingredients for Ginger Prawns

  • Prawns
  • Tomato Ketchup
  • Black pepper
  • Red Chili Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Green Chili Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Virgin Olive Oil
  • Coriander Leaves

Steps to make Ginger Prawns

  1. Add all ingredients in a bowl

    2. Wash and keep prawns in a bowl. In another bowl, add soya sauce, msg (optional), green chili
    sauce, red chilli paste, crushed pepper powder, tomato puree, sugar, tomato ketchup, a pinch of salt,
    and vinegar. Mix all ingredients.

  2. Saute onion and garlic paste

    3. Heat oil in a pan over medium flame and add onion paste along with garlic paste and cook until
    the raw flavour is gone.

  3. Saute the masala

    4. Next, add in the chopped ginger along with the masala mixture prepared in step 1. Cook this until
    oil separates. Adjust the consistency of the gravy as you like.

  4. Cook prawns in gravy

    5. Take another pan and add butter. When the butter has melted, add prawns and masala gravy,
    and cook it on a low flame until prawns are tender. Add chopped coriander leaves and serve it.
